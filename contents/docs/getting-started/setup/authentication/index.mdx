# Login and Signup

Before you can start using AcadZ, you'll need to log in or sign up using your Google account. Here’s how:

---

### Step 1: Open the App
1. Launch AcadZ on your device.
2. On the welcome screen, you’ll see two options: **Log In** and **Sign Up**.

![Welcome Screen](image-path)

---

### Step 2: Sign Up
1. If you're new to AcadZ, tap on **Sign Up**.
2. Select **Continue with Google**.
3. Choose the Google account you’d like to use with AcadZ.
4. Once authenticated, your account will be created, and you'll be taken to the home screen.

![Google Sign Up](image-path)

---

### Step 3: Log In
1. If you already have an account, tap **Log In**.
2. Select **Continue with Google**.
3. Choose the same Google account you used to sign up.
4. After authentication, you’ll be redirected to the home screen.

![Google Log In](image-path)

---

You’re now logged in and ready to explore AcadZ!
