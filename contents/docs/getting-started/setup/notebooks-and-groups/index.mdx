# Notebooks and Groups

AcadZ uses a flexible organizational system of **notebooks** and **groups**. Here’s how to get started:

---

### Step 1: Accessing the Dashboard
1. After logging in, you’ll land on the dashboard.
2. The dashboard shows your existing groups and notebooks.

![Dashboard](image-path)

---

### Step 2: Creating a New Notebook
1. Tap the **+ New** button on the dashboard.
2. Select **New Notebook**.
3. Enter a name for your notebook in the text box provided.
4. Tap **Create**. Your notebook will now appear on the dashboard.

![New Notebook](image-path)

---

### Step 3: Creating a New Group
1. Tap the **+ New** button on the dashboard.
2. Select **New Group**.
3. Enter a name for your group in the text box provided.
4. Tap **Create**. The group will appear on the dashboard.
5. You can add more groups or notebooks within this group.

![New Group](image-path)

---

### Step 4: Navigating Groups and Notebooks
- Tap on a group to view its contents (nested groups or notebooks).  
- Tap on a notebook to access its features like lecture recording, OCR scanning, and transcript management.

![Group and Notebook Navigation](image-path)

---

You’re now set to organize your study materials using notebooks and groups in AcadZ!
