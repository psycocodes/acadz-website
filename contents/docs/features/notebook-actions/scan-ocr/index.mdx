# Scanning Notes

AcadZ also allows you to scan your handwritten notes and convert them into text using OCR (Optical Character Recognition). Here’s how:

---

### Step 1: Open a Notebook
1. Navigate to the notebook where you’d like to store the scanned transcript.
2. Tap on the notebook to open it.

![Open Notebook](image-path)

---

### Step 2: Start Scanning
1. Inside the notebook, tap the **Scan** button.
2. The camera will open. Position the document in front of the camera and make sure it’s well-lit and in focus.

![Scan Button](image-path)

---

### Step 3: Capture the Image
1. Tap the **Capture** button to take a picture of the notes.
2. The app will process the image to extract text using OCR.

![Capture Image](image-path)

---

### Step 4: View and Edit the Transcript
1. Once the OCR process is complete, the app will display the extracted text as a transcript.
2. You can edit the transcript as needed.

![OCR Result](image-path)

---

### Step 5: Save the Transcript
1. After editing, tap **Save** to store the transcript in the notebook.

You’ve successfully scanned and converted your notes into text using AcadZ!
