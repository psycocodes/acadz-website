# Editing Flashcards

AcadZ allows you to edit flashcards for complete customization. You can modify, add, or delete flashcards to suit your study needs. Follow these steps:

---

### Step 1: Access the Flashcards
1. Open the notebook containing the transcript.
2. Tap on the transcript to open it.
3. Tap the **Flashcards** button to access the flashcards created from the transcript.

![Open Flashcards](image-path)

---

### Step 2: Edit Existing Flashcards
1. Tap on a flashcard to view its details.
2. Edit the **Question** or **Answer** field as needed.
3. Tap **Save** to update the flashcard.

![Edit Flashcards](image-path)

---

### Step 3: Add New Flashcards
1. Tap the **Add Flashcard** button at the top of the flashcard list.
2. Fill in the **Question** and **Answer** fields.
3. Tap **Save** to add the flashcard to the set.

![Add Flashcards](image-path)

---

### Step 4: Delete Flashcards
1. Long press on the flashcard you want to delete.
2. Tap **Delete** from the menu that appears.
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

![Delete Flashcards](image-path)
