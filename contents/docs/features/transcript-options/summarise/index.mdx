# Summarizing Transcripts

AcadZ can help you condense long transcripts into a concise summary with key points. Here’s how to summarize your transcript:

---

### Step 1: Open the Transcript
1. Navigate to the notebook that contains the transcript you want to summarize.
2. Tap on the notebook to open it.

![Open Notebook](image-path)

---

### Step 2: Access the Transcript
1. Inside the notebook, locate the transcript you wish to summarize.
2. Tap on the transcript to open it.

![Open Transcript](image-path)

---

### Step 3: Start the Summarization
1. Once the transcript is open, tap the **Summarize** button at the top.
2. The app will process the transcript and generate a summary.

![Summarize Button](image-path)

---

### Step 4: View the Summary
1. The app will display the summary, which will include:
   - A title
   - Bullet points with key takeaways
   - An introduction
   - A conclusion

![Summary Result](image-path)

---

### Step 5: Save or Edit the Summary
1. If you're satisfied with the summary, tap **Save** to store it in your notebook.
2. You can also edit the summary if needed.

You’ve successfully summarized your transcript using AcadZ!
